Bassett Hound

Bassets are scent hounds, meaning that they were bred to hunt by following the scent of their prey.

    Dog Breed Group: Hound Dogs

    Height: Up to 1 foot 2 inches tall at the shoulder

    Weight: 45 to 60 Pounds

    Lifespan: 10 to 12 years

Their short-legged appearance is the result of a form of dwarfism called achondroplasia. Despite his large size, the Basset believes he's a lap dog and will do his best to fit in yours.
